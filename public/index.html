Welcome to Xalpheria

Enter the digital realm of electronic music and immersive soundscapes. Explore my latest releases and dive into the thoughts that shape my sonic journey.

I do not use A.I prompted music. All of the music is created by me, and has been as such for the past 25 years. I draw from a wide range of influences and styles.

My album covers are created with generative A.I for now. They are place holders until I get around to commissioning some digital artwork. I totally suck at graphics.

If anyone does that sort of thing, please email me. I will pay you for your human creativity. As to the A.I elephant in the room, I take a nuanced but cautious view of it.

I'm a software developer by trade and have watched as it has slowly eroded my value as a dev. Graphic Designers are pretty much out of a job and so are online only musicians like myself

Udio and Suno have flooded spotify and have started to drown out real musicians and producers. Eventually, if the billionaire class isn't stopped I believe A.I will destroy us.

Conversly, if Gene Roddenberry were in charge of the A.I revolution, we could be living a utopia of unimaginable delight! It's all in how this stuff is directed.

It's all about the incentive structure and right now it's all about profit and not human beings. That is the conundrum of our age isn't it?
